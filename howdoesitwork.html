At Simply we understand your pain. We've talked to customers about the rewards and joy they get when their parents successfully learn how to send emails, video chat, or compose texts. We've also know how frustrating and time consuming it can be to get to this end point.

We're here to help. We have spent hours curating a robust library of tutorials (video, audio, text) which are specifically designed to help your parents learn how to use technology…simply!

STEP 1

Click Explore Tutorials to browse our hand-curated tutorials. Filter by Type, Format, Device and Average Rating. You can also preview the tutorial by clicking on the icons. Click ADD to add each tutorial to your list after preview.

STEP 2

Ready to send tutorial links? Just click on the Ready to Send button on the Explore Tutorials page to send the seleted tutorials to your loved ones. Register on Simply or sign into your account. Your account will save your tutorial history, contact information and personalized lessons.

STEP 3

Send a personalized greeting to your love ones along with the selected tutorial link(s).

Experience the joyful reward of making your parent or loved one comfortable, confident and excited about technology!
